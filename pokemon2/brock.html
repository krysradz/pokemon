Brock
"Twinleaf Champion"

Winner of the Twinleaf Battle Tournament for three years in a row, Brock shows his battle know-how by defeatingn opponents from all over Kanto. At one point, Brock was a Gym Leader, which lends to him being adept at strategizing during battle. Brock is the trainer for you if you want a more in-depth look at the history of each Pokémon
