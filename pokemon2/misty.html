Misty
"World Renown Water-Type Pokémon Trainer"

An avid swimmer and fisher, Misty is quite proficient in anything having to do with water. This is what has led her to be the greatest Pokémon water trainer in Kanto. Mild-mannered and well-versed, Misty will walk you through the basics of spotting, identifying, and catching Pokémon. She is not a one-trick pony; although her specialty is water Pokémon, she is more than familiar with catching all types of Pokémon.
